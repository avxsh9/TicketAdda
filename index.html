About TicketAdda

Our mission to make live entertainment accessible and safe for everyone in India.

Pioneering a Trusted Marketplace

Founded in 2025, TicketAdda set out to transform the way India buys and sells event tickets. We saw the gap — people needed a safe, transparent, and reliable place to trade tickets for concerts, sports matches, festivals, and more.
