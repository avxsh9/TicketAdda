About This Trek

Chopta, often called the ‚ÄúMini Switzerland of India,‚Äù is a stunning hill station located at an altitude of around 2,700 meters, known for its lush green meadows and breathtaking pastures. It serves as the base for several trekking routes, the most popular being the 9 km trek to Chandrashila Peak. On the way lies the sacred Tungnath Temple, the highest Shiva shrine in the world, which adds both spiritual and cultural significance to the journey. The combination of natural beauty, adventure, and spirituality makes Chopta a must-visit destination for trekkers and nature lovers alike.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Departure to Chopta

We'll depart for Chopta (Chandrashila route) by Tempo Traveller around 9:00 PM from Akshardham Metro / Mayur Vihar Metro station. Enjoy the overnight journey with fellow travellers.

Departure ~ 9:00 PM Tempo Traveller (Overnight)
Day 2

Reach Chopta

On the way visit Devprayag (sangam of Alaknanda & Bhagirathi). Arrive in Chopta, check in to Swiss camps, freshen up and rest. Explore Chopta meadows, take photos and enjoy nature. Dinner and overnight stay with bonfire & music (DJ if permitted).

Tungnath & Chandrashila

Wake up early to catch sunrise. After breakfast, drive to Tungnath trailhead and start the trek to Tungnath (one of the highest Shiva temples) and onward to Chandrashila Peak. Enjoy panoramic Himalayan views from the summit. Return to Chopta by evening. Dinner and overnight stay with bonfire & music.
