About This Trek

Embark on a spiritual and adventurous journey to Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world, located at an altitude of 3,680 meters. This 4-day trek takes you through lush meadows, ancient forests, and offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan peaks including Nanda Devi, Trishul, and Chaukhamba.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Rishikesh to Chopta

Drive from Rishikesh to Chopta (220 km, 8-9 hours). En route, visit Devprayag and enjoy scenic views. Overnight stay in Chopta camps.

220 km Drive Camp Stay
Day 2

Chopta to Tungnath to Chandrashila

Trek to Tungnath Temple (3.5 km, 3-4 hours) and further to Chandrashila Summit (1.5 km, 1-2 hours). Return to Chopta. Witness spectacular sunrise views.

10 km Trek 4,000m Altitude
Day 3

Chopta Exploration & Deoria Tal

Optional trek to Deoria Tal (2.5 km). Explore the beautiful meadows of Chopta. Evening bonfire and cultural activities.

5 km Trek Bonfire Night
Day 4

Chopta to Rishikesh

Morning leisure time. Drive back to Rishikesh with beautiful memories. Trek ends with drop at Rishikesh.

220 km Drive Trek End
